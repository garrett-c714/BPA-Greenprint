How They Work

A bicycle is a clean and healthy choice of transportation. The only energy being used is man made through the pedals and chain, so instead of burning fossil fuels, the rider burns calories. Also, it easily navigates through downtown traffic as most city roads have designated bike paths to keep cyclists safe.

Benefits And Savings

The main benefits that come along with owning a bike are environmental and health perks. The bike releases no emissions while using it for transport. Compared to a car which emits lots of carbon, the bike is a much more environmentally friendly option. In addition riding a bike rather than driving is great exercise, and will help you stay fit. While a bike obviously can't take you everywhere and is not as fast, using it from time to time on short commutes will help the environment, help you stay fit, and save you money on gas.
