Earthship Bioshelter

Information

An Earthship Bioshelter is a house made entirely out of natural and recycled materials. The household is entirely powered by solar panels found on its roof. The Earthship is insulated by recycled tires which regulate temperature year round. A water storage tank can be found in the basement of the Earthship. This system collects ground water, found way below the Earthâ€™s surface, and pumps it up to its storage tank. The greenhouse serves as the food source for the house. Earthships are very environmentally friendly in concept, but because of organizations such as HOAs banning them due to their visual state, it can take a lot of transportation to transport materials to the more remote locations. This transportation can take a negative toll on the environment, so it is better to build Earthships in communities with other Bioshelters.
